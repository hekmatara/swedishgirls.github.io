Swedish Girls

Available on

Instagram

An art- and design collective based in Stockholm. Together, the emerging artists Matilda Ellow, Julia Jondell, Mira Bergh and Josefin Zachrisson form Swedish Girls.

Swedish Girls challenge normative ways of working and have a conceptual focus. Their projects exist in the borderland of art and design and handle contemporary discussions to arouse feelings and continued conversations.

Alongside the collective every member has their individual practice, their work includes a wide range of collaborations, designs and exhibited artwork.

“Together and individually, we claim a space in an industry that needs us.” - Swedish Girls

About

Matilda Ellow is a Swedish artist and designer. Noted for her strong will and extremely well thought out style. Her line of work includes furniture design, products, set design and anything she sets her mind to. Ellow is known for her award winning degree work “Self Titled”, shown at Ung Svensk Form 2020. She is also head of Graphic design at Swedish Girls.

Julia Jondell is a Swedish artist and designer. Noted for her mind blowing sense of details and great focus. In her career, her main focus has been on smaller pieces with great personal attachment. About

Mira Bergh is a Swedish artist and designer. Noted for her ability to solve everything. Her work spans a range of styles, from furniture and sculptures, to set design and curating. Bergh is known for her furniture collection “Seats System” (https://www.seats-system.com) made together with Josefin Zachrisson and for co-funding the design network “Drip”.

About

Josefin Zachrisson is a Swedish artist and designer. Noted for her creativity that goes far beyond human ability. Her work spans a range of expressions from experimental to conceptual. During her career, she has released many top-ranked works such as the furniture collection “Seats System” (https://www.seats-system.com) with Mira Bergh and the scenography for the Swedish Grammy awards. Zachrisson is represented as a model at Mikas Stockholm and as an artist at MauMau collective.
