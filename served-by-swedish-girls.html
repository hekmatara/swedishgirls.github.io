Served by Swedish Girls

Presenting a group exhibition with Josefin Zachrisson, Matilda Ellow, Julia Jondell and Mira Bergh, all part of Swedish Girls. In their presentation Swedish Girls shows a selection of individual and common works in the borderland of art and design.

This exhibition is curated and produced by Slobodan Zivic. Initiated by Ingmari Pagenkemper for Cirkus establishment, Djurgården Stockholm.
